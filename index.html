About SEACET

Our History

Established in 2007 by the South East Asian Education Trust, SEACET has grown into a leading engineering institution in Bangalore. Affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and accredited by AICTE and NAAC B++, we are committed to academic excellence and innovation.

Leadership

Led by Principal Dr. B. Venkata Narayana (Ph.D.), SEACET fosters a culture of research and ethical values. His vision emphasizes industry collaboration and holistic student development.

Our Campus

Spread across 45 acres in K.R. Puram, Bangalore, our eco-friendly campus features modern infrastructure, Wi-Fi connectivity, and a vibrant academic environment. Key highlights include a 50,000+ book library, advanced labs, and sports facilities.

Admissions

Admission Process

  • Apply through KCET, COMED-K (B.E.), PGCET (M.Tech), or KMAT (MBA).
  • Submit 10+2/B.E. marksheets and entrance exam scores.
  • Attend counseling sessions for seat allocation.
  • Management quota seats available for direct admission.

Scholarships

Available for SC/ST/OBC, merit-based for top KCET/COMED-K ranks, and financial aid for economically weaker sections. Contact admission office for details.

Eligibility

B.E.: 45% in 10+2 (PCM). M.Tech: 50% in B.E./B.Tech. MBA: 50% in Bachelor’s. Diploma: 35% in SSLC. Application period: February–April.
